About the Conference

International Conference on Women in Innovation, Technology and Entrepreneurship, ICWITE is a flagship conference of the IEEE WIE AG, Bangalore Section, India. The very first edition of ICWITE will be held on 1st and 2nd December 2022. The conference will feature plenary talks, tutorials, workshops and invited papers by distinguished researchers and technologists as well as contributed papers from academics and industry professionals. ICWITE 2022 is a platform for women to showcase their expertise in Innovation, as technologists, researchers, entrepreneurship and industry leadership. It is an International Conference that aims to celebrate the achievements and accomplishments from across the globe. The conference enables them to share their ideas on emerging technologies and innovative solutions that can guide and lead towards a safer, sustainable and better tomorrow. The Conference will be held in a Hybrid Mode.



IEEE International Conference for Women in Innovation, Technology & Entrepreneurship (ICWITE 2022) has been created to provide opportunities to foster an ecosystem for innovation and entrepreneurship among the Women. This international conference attempts to celebrate and showcase women who are leaders in Innovation, Entrepreneurship, and Future Technology. The theme of the first edition of ICWITE 2022 will be “Engineering Sustainable Futures Responsibly”



The IEEE ICWITE 2022 Summit will be held at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India between 1st to 3rd December 2022. This is the flagship conference of IEEE WIE Bangalore Section AG. It will be held under the aegis of IEEE Bangalore Section & WIE, India Council. The conference will be held in Hybrid Mode.

ICWITE 2022 is designed with following tracks:

  • Women Entrepreneurs
  • Women in Technology
  • Women & SIGHT
  • Women in Innovation

The conference will include keynote addresses, panel discussions, patent, paper and poster presentations, tutorials, project demonstrations and many more innovative activities in the above listed tracks.



The conference, in its very first edition, aims to bring together leading organizations, institutions, start-ups, academicians and researchers to exchange and share their experiences and expertise on all aspects of “Future Technologies” and “Humanitarian” spheres. It also provides an interdisciplinary platform to present papers and discuss the recent innovations and challenges The conference is unique in that, we will be inviting scholars and practitioners to present their research/ work as posters, papers, and their patents. With the view to encourage participation of women scholars, each of these submissions should have at least one female author, preferably the first author. The accepted papers will be published in the IEEE Xplore if it meets the expected standards.

Paper/ Poster presentations will be made in topics across the following edge technologies, but not limited to:

  • AI/ ML, Blockchain
  • Computer Networks & Cybersecurity
  • Cloud, Big Data & 5G Communications
  • Quantum Computing
  • Devices, Materials & Processing
  • IOT, Sensor Technologies & Wearables
  • Digital Reality - AR/VR, Digital Twins
  • Smart Grids & Renewable Energy
  • Robotics, Control & Automation
  • Smart Cities & Infrastructure Technologies
  • FinTech, EduTech, AgriTech - Applications for Humanitarian Technology
